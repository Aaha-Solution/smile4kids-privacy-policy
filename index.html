Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to Smile4Kids. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information while ensuring a safe learning environment designed for children, in compliance with Google Play Families Policies.

1. Information We Collect

Smile4Kids does not collect any personal information from children. The app is designed so that all sensitive information, if any, is handled only by parents or guardians.

We do NOT collect from children:

  • Email
  • Password
  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • Photos or media
  • Location data
  • Any identifiers

Parent-Only Account Access

Some features of Smile4Kids allow parents/guardians to create an account for managing purchases, access settings, or tracking progress. Account creation is strictly for adults. Children cannot create an account or provide any personal information.

2. How We Use Information (Parents Only)

If parents choose to create an account, the provided information is used only for:

  • Authenticating parent login
  • Managing purchases or subscriptions
  • Providing customer support

All information is encrypted and used only for essential app functionality. No child data is stored.

3. No Ads, No Tracking, No Third-Party Sharing

Smile4Kids does not show ads, does not use tracking SDKs, and does not share data with third parties.

  • No advertising
  • No analytics tracking for children
  • No third-party cookies
  • No data selling or sharing

4. Child Safety & Compliance

Our app follows Google Play Families Policy, COPPA guidelines, and ensures:

  • No personal data is collected from children.
  • All sensitive actions are controlled only by parents.
  • Educational content is safe, age-appropriate, and free from harmful material.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure storage techniques to protect parent-provided information. Children’s data is never collected or stored.

6. Parental Controls

Parents may review or delete their account information at any time by contacting us. Children cannot access or modify account settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
