Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to Smile4Kids. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information while ensuring a safe learning environment designed for children, in compliance with Google Play Families Policies.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information Collected from Parents

To manage user accounts and provide app functionality, we may collect the following information from parents or guardians:

  • Name (for account personalization)
  • Email Address (for login and recovery)
  • Phone Number (for account authentication or support)
  • City/Region (for content localization)

B. Information NOT Collected from Children

Smile4Kids does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data entry is intended for parents only. We do NOT collect:

  • Precise Location (GPS)
  • Photos or Camera Data
  • Microphone Data (unless strictly for local voice lessons, never stored)
  • Biometrics

2. How We Use Parent Information

The information provided by parents is used strictly for:

  • Account Management: Creating and securing your parent account.
  • App Functionality: customizing the experience based on your city/region.
  • Communication: sending important updates or support responses.

We do not sell this data to third parties.

3. Child Safety & Parental Gate

Account creation is restricted to adults. We employ a "Parental Gate" (age verification or security question) to ensure that a child cannot mistakenly provide personal information.

4. No Ads & No Third-Party Tracking

Smile4Kids is ad-free. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or tracking tools that profile children.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Parents may request the deletion of their account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
